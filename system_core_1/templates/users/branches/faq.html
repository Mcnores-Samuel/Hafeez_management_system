How to Add Stock
  • Smartphones (with IMEI): Scan up to 30 IMEI numbers of the same model at once. Enter the cost per model, model name (as suggested), and supplier details before submitting.
  • Accessories: Enter the accessory name, model (from the package description), cost per item, and total quantity.
  • Refurbished Devices: Enter the device name, model, cost, and total quantity—same process as accessories.
  • Appliances: Follow the same process as accessories.
How to Register Sales

Sales on Yellow are automatically recorded, but cash sales must be done manually.

  • Yellow (Loan-Based) Sales: These are automatically tracked by the system.
  • Cash Sales: Must be manually recorded by the sales rep.
    • Scan or search for the IMEI number.
    • If found, click the "Sale on Cash" or "Deploy" button and follow the instructions.
How to Conduct Stock Taking

Go to Deploy Stock > Stock Taking, scan all available stock IMEIs, and submit.

How to Distribute Stock
  • Go to Deploy Stock and scan the IMEI numbers of the devices to be transferred.
  • Select the receiving shop.
  • Choose the deployment date and confirm the transfer.
How to Make Multiple Sales
  • Navigate to Deploy Stock > Sales.
  • Enter the IMEI numbers of the devices to be sold.
  • Choose the sales type (cash or loan).
  • Enter the sales date and confirm the sale.
How to View Available Stock
  • Navigate to Available Stock or My Stock.
  • View stock analysis or a detailed list of available inventory.
  • Use the search bar to find specific items.
How to View Sales
  • Navigate to the Sales section. The system displays the current month’s sales by default.
  • View a summary of all sales, including cash and loan-based sales.
  • Use the search bar to find specific sales.
  • Adjust the input fields to view sales from other months.
